FRIESIANS

Friesians are horse breed originating in Friesland in North Nertherland.

It has long arched neck, well-chiseled, short-ears and spanish type head.

This breed is classified as light draught horse.

Origin: Friesland

Height: 60 to 68 inches

Color: Black

Distinguish features: Black, powerfully muscled, agile with elegant action, thick mane and tail, feather on the lower legs
